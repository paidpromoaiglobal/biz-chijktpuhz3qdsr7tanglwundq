About Us

Giant Gymnastics Academy

Giant Gymnastics Academy is Coimbatore's dedicated gymnastics training centre, located in the heart of Gandhi Nagar, Subramaniapalayam. With a passion for developing athletic excellence and personal discipline, our academy offers structured gymnastics programs designed to nurture talent from the ground up. Whether you are a beginner taking your first tumble or an advanced gymnast pushing competitive boundaries, our expert coaches are committed to guiding every student with care and precision.

Nestled conveniently near Kandhavel Mahal in Gounder Mills, our facility is equipped with professional-grade apparatus and a safe, encouraging environment that motivates students to achieve their personal best. Open six days a week from early morning to late evening, we make it easy for families across Coimbatore to fit world-class gymnastics training into their busy schedules. Join our growing community of young champions and experience the Giant difference today!

  • Open 6 Days a Week from 5:30 AM to 9:30 PM for maximum scheduling flexibility
  • Expert Coaches dedicated to safe, progressive, and personalized gymnastics development
